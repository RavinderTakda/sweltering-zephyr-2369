Who We Are

Nice to meet you! We are a tech-driven online retailer located near Salt Lake City in
the shadow of the Wasatch Mountains. Since our beginnings in 1999, Overstock has
evolved from a fledging startup to a billion-dollar online retailer as a result of a hard-
working and creative team.

What We Value

We are passionate about doing our part to make life better. We provide high-quality
merchandise, great value, and exceptional customer service. We connect people
with products and services in new and unexpected ways. We do our part to create
dream homes for all.
